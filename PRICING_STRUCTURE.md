## Pricing Strategy

### Why These Prices?

1. **FREE Tier ($0)**
   - Low barrier to entry
   - Allows users to experience core value
   - Creates natural upgrade path at 5 employees

2. **GROWTH Tier ($29/month)**
   - Competitive pricing for small businesses
   - Significant value jump from Free (5x employees, AI features)
   - Sweet spot for most small businesses

3. **PRO Tier ($79/month)**
   - Premium pricing for larger organizations
   - ~6.7x employee capacity vs Growth (15 â†’ 100)
   - Advanced features justify higher price
   - Targets businesses with 15+ employees

### Feature Gating Strategy

**Free Tier Restrictions:**
- AI features blocked with upgrade prompts
- Analytics blocked with upgrade prompts
- CSV export blocked with upgrade prompts
- Custom roles blocked with upgrade prompts

**Growth Tier:**
- All core features unlocked
- Advanced AI recommendations still in Pro
- Advanced analytics still in Pro

**Pro Tier:**
- Everything unlocked
- Priority support
- Future premium features (SMS, API, etc.)
